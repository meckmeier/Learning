June-September (exact dates and locations vary)

Join Green and Healthy Schools this summer by attending a professional development event near you! Green & Healthy Schools is partnering with 18 regional leaders from across Wisconsin to host a series of 10 engaging educator workshops. These events will cover topics, including groundwater conservation, urban forestry, school gardening, site visits, sailing, and much more. Don't miss this chance to connect, learn, and bring fresh ideas back to your students! Visit the website for more information.

RECURRING EVENTS

Great Lakes Trek

Water, Aquatic Life, Geology, Plants, Wildlife, Human Connections

Join our Master Naturalist guides for an online trek focused on learning and exploring the Great Lakes — places that make Wisconsin a great place to live, work or play. You’ll be able to learn at your own pace using online readings, videos, and supplemental activities to reinforce the key concepts. Instead of book chapters, the trek uses an online network of trails to deliver accessible and fact-based knowledge. Find more information and registration here: https://wimasternaturalist.org/training/treks/great-lakes-trek/
